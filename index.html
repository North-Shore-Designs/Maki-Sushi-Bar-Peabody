About Maki

Maki Sushi Bar & Grill opened in Peabody, Massachusetts in 2010. Located at 43 Main Street in downtown Peabody, we offer over 50 specialty rolls, nigiri, sashimi, grilled entrees, and a full bar.

Awarded Editor's Choice and Readers' Choice for Best Sushi by Northshore Magazine (Best of the North Shore 2023), Readers' Choice 2021, and recognized by The Salem News.

Walk-ins are welcome, and reservations are available for parties of 6 or more.
